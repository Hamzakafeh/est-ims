01
How do I change or reset my password?
Go to your profile settings and click Change Password. Enter your current password followed by your new password, then confirm. If you've forgotten your password, use the Forgot Password link on the login page — a reset link will be sent to your registered email.
02
Why can't I log in to my account?
Ensure your credentials are correct and that Caps Lock is off. Your account may be temporarily locked after multiple failed attempts — wait 10 minutes and try again. If the issue persists, contact your system administrator or reach out via the Contact page.
03
How do I add a new product to inventory?
Navigate to Inventory → Products and click the + Add Product button. Fill in the required fields: product name, SKU, category, unit, and initial stock quantity. Once saved, the item will appear in all relevant reports and stock views.
04
How do stock alerts and low-quantity thresholds work?
Each product has a configurable Minimum Stock level. When current quantity drops at or below this threshold, the system flags the item in red on the dashboard and includes it in the Low Stock report. You can update thresholds from the product's edit page.
05
How do I export a report to PDF or Excel?
Open any report from the Reports section, then use the Export dropdown in the top-right corner. Choose PDF for printable formatted reports or Excel (.xlsx) for raw data you can further analyze. Date range filters apply to the exported file as well.
06
What browsers and devices are supported?
EST-iMs® is optimized for modern browsers: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Edge 90+, and Safari 14+. It is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome on a desktop.
