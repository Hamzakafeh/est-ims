How It Works

From first access to daily inventory management — five simple steps.

01
Create Account

Register from the welcome page with your employee details and submit an access request.

02
Get Approved

An admin reviews your request and activates your account with the appropriate zone access.

03
Log In

Sign in with your credentials to enter the system and access your assigned workspace.

04
Choose Zone

Select your warehouse zone — each zone holds its own inventory categorized by year and month.

05
Manage Inventory

Track items, scan QR codes for quick lookups, edit stock, and export reports to Excel.
